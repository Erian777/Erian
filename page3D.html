Riding Section

For the riding section you must be able to:

-Mount and dismount,
-Have correct position in saddle,
-Hold the reins correctly,
-Ride without lead, walk, trot,
-Rise to the trot,
-Simple aids; Walk to halt,
-Walk to trot,
-Turn at walk,
-Show simple exercises at halt to walk,
-Reasons for exercises,
-Learn easy games,
-Praise a pony,
-And the do's and dont's on the road.


Horse Management Section.


For horse manegement section you must be able to:

-Learn how to aproach and handle a horse,
-Catching and putting on halter,
-Turning pony out in paddock,
-Giving pony a treat,
-How to tie up correctly,
-How to lead and turn correctly,
-Washing ponies bit and brushing ponies over,
-Naming the points of a pony,
-Points of saddle,
-And points of bridle.
