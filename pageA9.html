Riding Section


-Turnout of Horse & Rider to Competition Standard
-Have a deep, supple seat on flat & be able to display aids
-Sound knowledge of training terms and of Scales of Training
-Show correct paces & movement with horse on the bit
-Perform following movements:
-Medium trot and canter
-Demi-pirouette at walk
-Simple change of leg
-Counter canter
-Rein back
-Lateral work in trot including shoulder in
-Be able to describe req of & understand how to prepare horse for
-leg yield, shoulder-in, travers and half pass
-Know the principles of handling,lungeing,backing & riding young horses
-Show how to age a horse by its teeth

JUMPING


-Show a strong, correct & adaptable position over SJ's & XC fences
-Sound knowledge of use of gymnastic exercises to improve a
-horses jumping & of building fences and courses
-Ride & jump horses of any type, tempremt or stage of training
-Assess the horses potential, present perf & how could be improved
-Ride pace work correctly

COACHING


-Show a record of a minimum of 10 teaching hours at Pony Club rallies
-Produce a rally plan suitable for own branch, covering 3 diff levels

KNOWLEDGE OF PONY CLUB


-Knowledge of PC Fair Play charter
-Knowledge of NZPCA – its formation, objective & organisation
-Knowledge of the running of your own Club
-Know the names of your branch, club and area officials
