C+ Certificate

Requirements

Index D D+ C B A

Riding Section:



-Turnout, horse & rider
-Show correct Position, walk, trot, canter, gallop
-Position becoming steadier & rider progressing on sound lines
-Show sitting trot, with and without stirrups
-Show rising trot on either diagonal, change of diagonal
-Understanding horse actions, foot falls, rhythm in all paces
-Show practical knowledge of even paces and horse accepting bit
-Know what is meant by “on the bit”
-Knowledge of aids for and carry out: Circles: 10m walk
-15m trot
-20m canter
-Serpentines and loops
-Show a few lengthened strides at trot
-Show turn on forehand
-Show change of leg thru trot
-Begin to practice leg yielding at walk and trot
-Show correct position and control at gallop
-Show that the jumping position better established
-understand what is meant by correct contact in jumping.
-Ride correctly over trotting poles – ground or raised
-Jump a variety of fences and simple comb at trot & canter 80cm
-Ride up and down hills, drop fences, banks, ditches
-know how to walk a jumping course

Knowledge of Pony Club



-Have knowledge of Pony Club
-Know the name of area delegate to COM and Area President
-Fair Play Charter

Road Code, Riding Out & About



-knowledge of Road code as applied to horses
-Show understanding riding alone, company, roads, country

HORSE MANAGEMENT

Paddocking



-Looking for daily – horse and paddock
-Recognise poisonous plants

Feeding, exercising, conditioning



-Understanding the principles of water and feeding
-Recognise good and unsuitable hay
-Recognise feedstuffs as listed
-Principles of getting horse fit
-Describe mounts feed programme – diff times of year

Handling


-Show how to handle safely and effectively all times
-Lead horse in and out of yards, gate

Grooming and clipping


-Show how to groom effectively
-Know name and use of types of clips
-Know how to prepare horse for clipping
-Discuss cooling down after work etc

Foot & Shoeing


-Name parts of foot and shoe
-Know the name of farriers tools and uses
-Describe own mounts shoeing and type used

Travelling


-Show how to put travel boots/bandages
-Show how to load and unload horse
-Explain how to care for horse during journey & away from home

Saddlery & Equipment


-Be able to fit saddle & snaffle bridle
-Type of 3 bits of choice
-Demonstrate care and cleaning of equipment
-Health & First Aid
-All in Manual 1 – Health, Ailments & Injuries
-How to detect heat, pain and swelling in leg
-Know when to call the vet and what to have on hand
-Know how to carry out prescribed treatments
-Internal parasites affecting horses
-Describe mounts worming programme
