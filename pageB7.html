Riding Section


-Turnout, horse and rider
-Position all paces
-Knowledge of physical exercises
-Diagonals correct all of the time
-Ride without stirrups inc canter
-Horse action, footfalls, rhythm all paces
-Show wkg trot, canter, diff accptg & on bit
-Circle 10m walk and trot
-Circle 15m canter
-Serpentine
-Lengthen stride in trot & Canter
-Riding in position (flexion)
-Leg yielding
-Canter from walk
-Change of leg thru trot
-Shallow canter loop w/out change leg
-Show progress to shoulder-in
-Explain aids for each movement and obj
-Parts of double bridle or pelham
-Ride using double bridle walk, trot
-Gallop
-Ride & jump diff horse & assessment
-Balanced jump position
-Importance of correct contact & Demo
-Jump variety fences & combinations up to 95cm
-Jump up, down hill, drops, banks, ditches
-Poles & exercise to improve horse jumping
-Basic knowledge of building jumps
-Know how to walk and assess jump course

Knowledge of Pony Club


-Fair Play Charter
-NZPCA President, National Coach
-Knowledge of structure & Org of NZPCA
-Objectives of NZPCA
-Responsibilities of a PC member

Horse Management

Paddocking


-Daily check horse & Paddock
-Recognise more common poisonous plants
-Knowledge of maintenance of own paddock

Feeding, Exercising, Conditioning


-Principles of feeding & watering
-Recognise good & unsuitable hay (3 types)
-Recognise feedstuffs & uses
-Merits of Commercially formulated feeds
-Principles of conditioning & getting horse fit
-Signs of fitness in a horse
-Bringing up after spell & roughing off
-Own mounts feeding at diff stages of fitness

Grooming, clipping & Trimming


-Understand term quartering & Strapping
-Strap horse efficiently
-Describe how to pull mane
-Show how to plait mane and tail
-Show how to trim heels with scissors & comb
-Names & uses of diff types of clip
-Describe how to prepare horse for clipping

Stabling


-List points to check in loosebox
-Stable tools muck out, pick droppings, bed down
-Knowledge of bedding: straw, sawdust, shavings
-Discuss stable routine over limited period
-What to look for when inspecting stabled horse
-Show how to lead horse in & out of loosebox
-Describe how to deal with Cast horse
-Describe how to deal with horse who has broken out

Saddlery & Equipment


-Check fitting of sadd, mtgle, noseband, bplate,sourcing
-Types and fitting of bshg boots, bell bts,badges, covers
-Discuss types of bits and action
-Discuss care, cleaning, storage of equipment
-Show how you'd inspect saddlery for soundness

Travelling & Bandaging


-Prepare horse for travel
-Practical knowledge loading/unloading from truck

Handling


-handle horse safely & efficiently all times
-Demonstrate Desc holding for showing, vet, clipping.
-Demonstrate runner-up in hand for lameness or action

The Foot & Shoeing


-Name parts of the Foot
-Name parts of shoe & diff types of shoes
-recognise good and bad shoeing
-Discuss own horses shoeing & reasons for shoes used

Health


-Sound knowledge of Manual 1
-Normal temp, pulse, resp. Assess respiration
-Know when to call vet & what to have on hand
-Know how to report sick/injured horse symptoms to vet
-Carry out prescribed treatment for wounds, lame, ailments
-Have some knowledge of internal parasites in horses
-Describe own mounts worming programme
-Discuss/demo knowledge of tying up
-Discuss/demo knowledge of sprains and splints

Conformation


-Discuss good & bad points of conf, know common terms
-Discuss good & bad action. Recognise faults.
-Have knowledge of horses teeth (names of teeth)
-Recognise permanent, milk teeth and tushes/
